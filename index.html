Post X

PostX is the latest development by Practikality Inc. It is a novel post generator and works simply by taking in the general requirements for your post and generating an aesthetically provoking text post which you can share with all your friends and family. Get it today from the Play Store.

Healr.

Healr is a platform that enables people to sell pharmaceuticals which have expired or have been left untouched and get credits in return. The user can then avail gift cards or vouchers from it. Healr has the potential to reduce medicinal waste and lower the wealth that goes to trash. Get it today from the Play Store.
